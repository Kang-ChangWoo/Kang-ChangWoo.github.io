About

I am a Ph.D. student in the 3D Vision & Robotics Lab at UNIST, advised by Prof. Kyung-Don Joo. My research focuses on 3D shape generation and multimodal sensor fusion using event cameras. I aim to design models that combine RGB-trained priors with event-based cues for low-latency, robust 3D perception under dynamic and low-light environments.
